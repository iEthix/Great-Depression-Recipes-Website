Egg Drop Soup Recipe (Great Depression Edition)

Whisk your way back to simpler times with this comforting Great Depression-inspired Egg Drop Soup.

A humble blend of nourishing broth, delicate ribbons of egg, and a sprinkle of fresh chives, this soup offers a heartwarming embrace in every spoonful.

Quick to prepare and light on the wallet, itâ€™s a timeless classic that brings the cozy nostalgia of home-cooking to your table.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons cold water
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 green onion, sliced

Recipe Instructions:

  1. In a large saucepan, bring broth to a boil over medium heat. Combine cornstarch and water until smooth; gradually stir into broth. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
  2. Reduce heat. Drizzle beaten egg into hot broth, stirring constantly. Remove from the heat; stir in onion.
