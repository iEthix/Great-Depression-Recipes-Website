Creamy Chipped Beef Fondue (Great Depression Edition)

Corn Dog Casserole is a playful twist on the traditional corn dog, transforming it into a hearty, one-dish meal.

It combines chopped hot dogs and sweet corn kernels mixed into a savory cornbread base.

Typically topped with a layer of cheese and baked until golden, this casserole is a family-friendly dish that brings the flavors of a classic fairground treat right to the dinner table.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups thinly sliced celery
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1-1/2 cups sliced green onions
  • 1-1/2 pounds hot dogs
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1-1/2 cups 2% milk
  • 2 teaspoons rubbed sage
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 packages (8-1/2 ounces each) cornbread/muffin mix
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided

Recipe Instructions:

  1. In a small skillet, saute celery in butter 5 minutes. Add onions; saute until vegetables are tender, 5 minutes longer.
  2. Place in a large bowl; set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 400Â°.
  4. Cut hot dogs into 1/2-inch slices.
  5. In the same skillet, saute hot dogs until lightly browned, about 5 minutes; add to vegetables. Reserve 1 cup.
  6. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, milk, sage and pepper.
  7. Add remaining hot dog mixture.
  8. Stir in cornbread mixes.
  9. Add 1-1/2 cups cheese.
  10. Spread into a shallow 3-qt. or 13x9-in. baking dish.
  11. Top with reserved hot dog mixture and remaining cheese.
  12. Bake, uncovered, until golden brown, about 30 minutes.
